Evaluate func on the joint predictive distribution for samples (integrating over the prior for the gamma rate) and aggregate the results using aggregate.

Parameters
[in]samplesThe weighted samples.
[in]funcThe function to evaluate.
[in]aggregateThe function to aggregate the results of func.
[in]isNonInformativeTrue if the prior is non-informative.
[in]offsetThe constant offset of the data, in particular it is assumed that samples are distributed as Y - "offset", where Y is a gamma distributed R.V.
[in]likelihoodShapeThe shape of the likelihood for samples.
[in]priorShapeThe shape of the gamma prior of the rate parameter of the likelihood for samples.
[in]priorRateThe rate of the gamma prior of the rate parameter of the likelihood for samples.
[out]resultFilled in with the aggregation of results of func.
