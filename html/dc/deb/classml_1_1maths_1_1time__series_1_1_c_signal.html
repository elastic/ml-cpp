Cooley-Tukey fast DFT transform implementation.

Note
This is a simple implementation radix 2 DIT which uses the chirp-z idea to handle the case that the length of fx is not a power of 2. As such it is definitely not a highly optimized FFT implementation. It should be sufficiently fast for our needs.
