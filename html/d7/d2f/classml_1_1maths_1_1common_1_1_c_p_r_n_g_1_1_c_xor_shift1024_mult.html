Detailed Description

The xorshift1024* pseudo-random number generator.

DESCRIPTION:
A period pseudo-random number generator with v.good empirical statistical properties.

Note that the three lowest bits of this generator are LSFRs, and thus they are slightly less random than the other bits. Use a sign test to extract a random Boolean value.

See also
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xorshift#cite_note-vigna2-9.
