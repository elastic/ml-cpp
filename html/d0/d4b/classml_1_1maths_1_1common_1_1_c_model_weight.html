Detailed Description

Represents the probability assigned to a model in the mixture.

Internally the log of weights are stored to allow weights to become very small without underflow. When weights are retrieved in normalized form any weight which is small w.r.t. the largest weight (i.e. less than double_eps * largest weight) is effectively zero and the corresponding model is (temporarily) removed from the collection.
