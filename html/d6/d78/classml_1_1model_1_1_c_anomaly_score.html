Compute a joint anomaly score for a collection of probabilities.

The joint anomaly score is assigned pro-rata to each member of the collection based on its anomalousness.

Parameters
[in]jointProbabilityWeightThe weight to assign the joint probability.
[in]extremeProbabilityWeightThe weight to assign the extreme probability.
[in]minExtremeSamplesThe minimum number of samples to include in the extreme probability calculation.
[in]maxExtremeSamplesThe maximum number of samples to include in the extreme probability calculation.
[in]maximumAnomalousProbabilityThe largest probability with non-zero anomaly score.
[in]probabilitiesA collection of probabilities for which to compute an aggregate probability and total score.
[out]overallAnomalyScoreFilled in with the overall anomaly score.
[out]overallProbabilityFilled in with the overall probability.
