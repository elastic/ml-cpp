Calculate an anomaly score for a collection of independent samples from the variable.

Parameters
[in]calculationThe style of the probability calculation (see maths_t::EProbabilityCalculation for details).
[in]weightStyleControls the interpretation of the weight that is associated with each sample. See maths_t::ESampleWeightStyle for more details.
[in]samplesA collection of samples of the variable. Each pair is the sample and weight, i.e. where is sample and is the weight of that sample.
[out]resultFilled in with the total anomaly score of samples.
