Detailed Description

template<EPartOfSpeech SPECIAL_PART1, std::size_t EXTRA_WEIGHT1, std::size_t DEFAULT_EXTRA_WEIGHT, std::size_t ADJACENT_PARTS_BOOST>
class ml::core::CWordDictionary::CWeightOnePartAdjacentBoost< SPECIAL_PART1, EXTRA_WEIGHT1, DEFAULT_EXTRA_WEIGHT, ADJACENT_PARTS_BOOST >

Functor for weighting one type of dictionary word by a certain amount and all dictionary words by a different amount, giving a boost to the weight when 3 or more dictionary words are adjacent one another.
