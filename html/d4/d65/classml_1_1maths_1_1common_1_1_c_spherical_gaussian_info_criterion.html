Detailed Description

template<typename POINT, EInfoCriterionType TYPE>
class ml::maths::common::CSphericalGaussianInfoCriterion< POINT, TYPE >

Computes the information content of a collection of point clouds under the assumption that they are distributed as a weighted sum of spherically symmetric Gaussians.
