Detailed Description

Manages the decay rate based on the data characteristics.

DESCRIPTION:
We can use estimates of the prediction errors to understand if our models are capturing the time varying components of a time series and if there has recently been a significant change from the time series behavior. In particular, we look at

  1. The ratio of the prediction bias to the prediction error.
  2. The ratio of the recent absolute to the long term prediction error.

If there is a significant bias in our predictions then our model is failing to capture some time varying component of the time series and the best we can do is to remember less history. If the short term and prediction error is large compared to the long term prediction error then the system has recently undergone some state change and we should re-learn the model parameters as fast as possible.
