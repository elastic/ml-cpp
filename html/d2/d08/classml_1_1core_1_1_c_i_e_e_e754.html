◆ EPrecision

enum ml::core::CIEEE754::EPrecision

Enumeration of possible precision types:

  1. Half precision: 10 bit mantissa, 5 bit exponent, 1 sign bit.
  2. Single precision: 23 bit mantissa, 8 bit exponent, 1 sign bit.
  3. Double precision: 52 bit mantissa, 11 bit exponent, 1 sign bit.

◆ round()

double ml::core::CIEEE754::round ( double value,
EPrecision precision )
static

This emulates rounding to a specified precision (based on the number of bits in the mantissa corresponding to precision). It uses round to nearest ties away from zero. It never converts the values to less than double precision so leaves the exponent unmodified and handles the case that the exponent would overflow.
