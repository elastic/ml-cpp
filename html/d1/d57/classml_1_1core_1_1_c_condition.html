Detailed Description

Wrapper around pthread_cond_wait.

DESCRIPTION:
Wrapper around pthread_cond_wait.

IMPLEMENTATION DECISIONS:
On Windows, condition variables are preferred to events, as they do not consume system handles, and match the semantics of pthread condition variables.
