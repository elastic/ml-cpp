Detailed Description

A view into the model details.

DESCRIPTION:
This defines the interface to extract various details of the CAnomalyDetectorModel to avoid cluttering the CAnomalyDetectorModel interface. The intention is to expose all aspects of the mathematical models of both individual and population models for visualization purposes.
